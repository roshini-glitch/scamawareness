Scam Awareness Singapore

Our Thoughts on Scams and Public Action

Scams not only cause financial loss but also emotional distress and loss of trust. As scams become more sophisticated, public awareness and education are vital.

The public can take steps to protect themselves:

  • Stay informed about latest scam trends.
  • Verify information independently before acting.
  • Report suspicious activities immediately to authorities.
  • Educate family and friends, especially vulnerable groups like the elderly.

Together, we can reduce the impact of scams and protect our communities.
