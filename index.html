Introduction to Cyber Scams in Singapore

How Common is Getting Scammed in Singapore

Scams are alarmingly common in Singapore. In 2023 alone, victims lost over S$47 million to scams. There were 1,152 reported cases, with many involving online platforms like dating apps and social media.

How to Avoid Being Scammed

  • Never disclose personal information, bank details, or passwords over the phone.
  • Always verify caller identity independently by calling official numbers.
  • Be suspicious of urgent demands for money transfers or immediate action.
  • Remember that real authorities will never ask you to transfer money.
