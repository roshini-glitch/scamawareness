Introduction to Cyber Scams in Singapore

Cyber scams have surged sharply in Singapore, causing devastating financial losses. In recent years, millions of dollars have been lost to phone scams and cyber fraud, with some victims losing over $200,000.

Scammers use various strategies including impersonation and sophisticated love scams. It's important to understand these scams to protect yourself and your loved ones.

Both the uneducated and even educated professionals fall prey to these deceptive tactics. The Singapore Police and scamalert.sg provide useful resources and tips on scam prevention.
