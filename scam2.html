Love Scam Joyce

Love scams target victims by building fake romantic relationships online, gaining trust and eventually requesting money.

How It Works

  • Scammers create fake profiles on dating sites or social media.
  • They groom victims by sharing personal stories and love messages.
  • Once trust is established, they ask for money citing emergencies or travel costs.

How to Avoid

  • Be cautious of people who avoid meeting in person or video calls.
  • Never send money or personal details to someone you haven't met.
  • Verify suspicious stories independently.

Real Story:

A woman lost over $100,000 to a scammer she met on social media who claimed to be a businessman stranded overseas. Always stay alert.
